About

I'm a graduating BME senior at USC transitioning into an Applied Mathematics master's, with the goal of pursuing a PhD in Operations Research. My research interests sit at the intersection of probability theory, stochastic control, and computational methods.

My background spans biomedical engineering, neural implant research, and independent work in financial mathematics. A recurring thread across my projects is the connection between ill-posed inverse problems — CT image reconstruction, volatility surface calibration — and the regularization techniques that make them tractable.
